The SYFC Story: Video compilation

We look back with wonder at how the Lord has led SYFC as a youth movement since 1957. Through this video compilation of these decades , we seek to count our blessings and celebrate His faithfulness. We have done down memory lane, contacted many who were once a part of this work, listened to their heartening stories, and put together this collective account of our history. Rejoice with us in the Lord’s providence and mercies as you recall your times and experiences in SYFC, remember ex-YFCers you served with and learn about His continuing ministry of youth evangelism through SYFC today! We are grateful to the Lord for His faithfulness and grace in the past, and we look forward to His continued leading in the future. We hope that this video will inspire you to continue to serve Him and to share the good news of Jesus Christ with others.
