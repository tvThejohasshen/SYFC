Ministries
Ministries

As John 15:5 reminds us to depend on God as we do the work of youth evangelism, we have named our ministries with the following acronyms:


C O R E

“Rooted in Christ, Reaching the Next Generation.We abide in Jesus as the true vine, drawing strength, wisdom, and direction from Him. From this place of dependence, we are empowered to serve, teach, engage, and evangelize—reaching young people where they are, nurturing their faith, and walking with them into a life fully rooted in Christ.”

S T E M
Support, Teaching & Engagement Ministries

In this ministry, staff seek to provide administrative support, equipping with teaching of God’s word as well as engagement of our friends & donors.

E D G E
Evolving & Digital Generation Evangelism Ministry

We befriend youths through particular interests, like: dance, arts and sports, or inclination through: school-based programs, community contact and church partnership.

Our Immersive Experience

Join us in experiencing meaningful connections, real stories, and life-changing encounters that bring faith to life for today’s digital generation.

Preteens

"Our mission is to nurture the spiritual and personal growth of pre-teens, guiding them toward faith, values, and a strong connection with their community through Christ-centered events and teachings.We believe that parents play a vital role in the spiritual growth of their children. Our ministry encourages regular communication and involvement, providing resources and support to help parents guide their preteens in their faith journey".

  • Support pre-teens in developing strong values and faith-based decisions.
  • Provide Christ-centered events to foster personal and spiritual growth.
  • Encourage a supportive community for pre-teens and their families.
  • Offer resources for parents to engage in their children’s faith journey.
  • Empower parents to play an active role in their child's spiritual development.

"We are here to help preteens grow in faith and parents to be empowered in guiding them".

Secondary School Ministry

"Reaching secondary school and college youths for Christ"

Bible Studies: Engaging in regular sessions to guide students in faith.
Peer Fellowship: Encouraging a supportive and fun environment.
Leadership Development: Opportunities for students to take leadership roles and grow.
Community Outreach: Helping teens serve others in meaningful ways.

Junior College Ministry

"We aim to help students navigate their faith during this critical period of their lives. We provide a supportive community, engaging discussions, and opportunities for personal growth and service".
Bible Studies: Deepening the understanding of faith during a crucial stage of life.
Mentorship: Personalized guidance to help students navigate both academic and spiritual challenges.
Fellowship Events: Building a supportive and encouraging environment for JC students.
Campus Outreach: Opportunities for students to share their faith and serve the community.

Youth Guidance Ministry

"Reaching youths from needy families, facing tremendous stress, displaying behavioural problems in school or at home and requiring some form of academic or social assistance".

The Youth Guidance ministry reaches out to at-risk youth and pre-teens. Some of the youth/pre-teens may come from dysfunctional or disadvantaged families, while others could be youth offenders struggling with addiction or associating with gangs and crimes.

Youth Guidance (YG) strives to guide and nurture these youth from:

  • Rehabilitation Centre
  • Residential Homes
  • Community outreach
  • Partnership with other social services or VWOs

"We share God’s love and comfort, teaching Christian values and life skills in the process. We use practical aids, games and sports, experiential learning, and academic help".

Polytechnic and ITE Ministry

"Reaching out to polytechnic and ITE students to share the love of Christ, providing guidance and support during their crucial years of study and personal development".

This ministry focuses on youth in post-secondary institutions, particularly polytechnics and ITE, who are navigating the challenges of academic pressure, career choices, and personal growth. Through mentorship, community building, and faith-based activities, we aim to guide them towards a fulfilling life in Christ.

The Polytechnic and ITE Ministry works to:

  • Provide spiritual guidance and support to students facing academic and personal challenges
  • Help students grow in their faith through Bible studies, fellowship, and community outreach
  • Organize events and activities that foster a deeper relationship with Christ and their peers
  • Partner with other Christian organizations and local churches to create opportunities for service and engagement
