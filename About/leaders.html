About Our Leaders

At Singapore Youth For Christ (SYFC), our leadership plays a vital role in guiding the mission, vision, and strategic direction of our ministry. The leaders, comprising our Board of Directors and Executive Leadership Team (ELT), bring together diverse expertise, faith-driven passion, and years of experience to serve youth and communities with integrity and compassion.

  • Upholding our mission to reach and empower young people in Singapore.
  • Maintaining accountability and transparency in all areas of our ministry.
  • Strategizing sustainable growth for impactful youth outreach programs.
  • Fostering unity and collaboration across all departments and ministries.

The team oversees spiritual, operational, and organizational aspects, ensuring that every decision reflects our values, promotes Christ-centered living, and inspires transformation among the next generation. Their servant-hearted leadership is what strengthens our foundation and propels us forward with purpose.
