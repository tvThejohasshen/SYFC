At SYFC, we are committed to nurturing young people in their faith and providing opportunities for spiritual growth, leadership, and service. When you join us, you'll become part of a vibrant community making a real impact in the world.


Spiritual Growth

Grow in your faith and relationship with Christ through weekly gatherings, Bible studies, and prayer sessions. Join a community where you can learn, share, and deepen your understanding of the Word of God.

Leadership Development

Develop your leadership skills by taking on active roles within the group. Lead Bible studies, mentor younger students, and plan community outreach initiatives that make a lasting difference.

Mission Trips

Embark on life-changing mission trips to different parts of the world. Serve communities in need and spread the message of hope and love through practical service and cultural exchange.>


How to Join


Joining us is easy! Follow these simple steps to become a member of the SYFC community:


Step 1: Fill Out the Application Form

Start your journey with SYFC .This will help us understand your background and how you want to contribute to our community.

Step 2: Attend an Orientation

Join us for an orientation session where you’ll meet other students, learn about SYFC's values and mission, and discover how you can get involved in our activities.

Step 3: Get Connected

Once you've completed the orientation, connect with other members through Bible study groups, prayer circles, and various outreach projects. Start serving and growing alongside others!


What’s in it for You?


When you become part of the SYFC family, you gain more than just friends. You gain a place to grow spiritually, develop essential life skills, and make a positive impact in the world. Here are some benefits of joining us:


Build Lifelong Friendships

At SYFC, you’ll be surrounded by people who share your faith and passions. You’ll form lasting relationships that go beyond just your youth group and into your lifelong journey with Christ.

Make an Impact

From local outreach to international mission trips, you will have numerous opportunities to serve others and make a lasting impact on communities in need.

Grow as a Leader

Develop your leadership potential through mentorship, training, and hands-on experience. Take on active roles that help shape the future of our youth ministry.

Bootstrap SYFC
  • "Empowering the next generation to live with purpose, faith, and love through Jesus Christ."
  • "Building a community of young people who are passionate about making a difference in the world."
  • "Inspiring youth to embrace their identity in Christ and impact their communities positively."
