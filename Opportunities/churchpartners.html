Church Partners

Working Together to Reach the Youth for Christ


Why Partner with Churches?

Singapore Youth for Christ (SYFC) believes that collaboration with local churches is key to transforming lives and building a Christ-centered generation. The youth are the future of the Church, and by working together, we can nurture, guide, and empower them spiritually and socially.

SYFC complements the church’s ministry by organizing youth camps, school outreaches, discipleship programs, and leadership development platforms. These programs are designed not to replace, but to strengthen and extend the church's influence into schools and communities.

Our Common Vision

Our partnership with churches is built on a shared vision — to see young people encounter Jesus, grow in their faith, and serve with purpose. We work hand-in-hand to:

  • Provide outreach opportunities in schools and neighborhoods
  • Train youth leaders and mentors for discipleship
  • Organize inter-church youth events and rallies
  • Foster a supportive network between youth ministries

Together, we build a kingdom mindset in the next generation.

Our Current Church Partners

We are blessed to journey alongside churches from various denominations and communities across Singapore. Some of our committed partners include:

  • Faith Community Baptist Church
  • Grace Assembly of God
  • Yishun Christian Church (Anglican)
  • Cornerstone Community Church
  • Church of Our Saviour
  • City Harvest Church

These churches have opened doors for SYFC to engage youth through cell groups, events, and community outreach efforts.

Testimonies from Pastors

"SYFC has been a valuable partner in discipling our youth and equipping our leaders. Their heart for evangelism and excellence in youth ministry has helped us reach more young people than we could alone." – Rev. Daniel Lim, Grace Assembly

"Partnering with SYFC expanded our outreach capacity, especially in schools. Their passionate and Christ-centered team works well with our youth ministry to cultivate meaningful connections." – Ps. Sharon Tan, Faith Community Baptist Church

Want to Partner with Us?

If you're a church leader or part of a youth ministry looking to collaborate, SYFC welcomes your partnership! We offer:

  • Customizable youth training programs
  • Event collaboration and support
  • Leadership mentorship and youth speaker engagements
  • Access to school outreach platforms and resources

Let’s join hands to bring hope, truth, and transformation to Singapore’s youth.
